Facts And Figures

  • Which batsman has scored the most number of centuries?

    Sachin Tendulkar has the most number of centuries internationally , 100 centuries ! Thats the reason he is considered the god of cricket !

  • Sanath Jeyasurya has more ODI wickets than Shane Warne !

    This is one of the most surprising fact that the legendary bowler Shane Warne has lesser wickets than Sanath Jeyasurya ! What a great fact to know !

  • Which bowler has the most number of wickets ever in the cricket history?

    The leading wicket-taker of all time in international tests is Sri Lanka's Muttiah Muralitharan. The spin bowler took 800 wickets in a test career spanning from 1992 to 2010.

  • Which batsmen has hitted the most number of sixes in ODI ?

    Shahid Afridi - 351 6s !

  • Which cricketer is the highest paid i the history of International Cricket ?

    Virat Kohli - 28 Million Dollars ! !

  • Which wicket keeper has most number of stumpings in international cricket ?

    With a total of 123 stumpings, Dhoni is leading the list of wicketkeepers with most number of stumpings to their name in the 50-over format. Dhoni is followed by Sri Lanka's Kumar Sangakkara (99 stumpings) and Romesh Kaluwitharana (75) stumpings.
